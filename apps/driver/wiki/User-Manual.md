## Trip Management

### Adding a New Trip

1. **Navigate to Trips Tab**
2. **Tap the + Button**
3. **Fill in Trip Details:**

**Required Fields:**

- Trip Number
- Truck Number
- Trip Date

**Optional Fields:**

- Trailer Number
- Pickup Locations (tap + to add multiple)
- Delivery Locations (tap + to add multiple)
- Border Crossing
- Notes
- Official Use field
- Photos

4. **Save the Trip**

### GPS Distance Tracking

When adding locations:

1. Tap the location icon
2. Allow location permissions
3. App calculates distance automatically
4. Distance shown in your preferred unit (mi/km)

### Editing a Trip

**Method 1: Swipe Right**

- In any trip list, swipe right on a trip
- Tap "Modify"
- Edit details and save

**Method 2: Tap to Expand**

- Tap a trip card to expand
- Tap edit icon
- Make changes and save

### Deleting a Trip

**Swipe Left:**

- Swipe left on any trip
- Tap "Delete"
- Confirm deletion

**Note**: Deleted trips cannot be recovered!

## Fuel Tracking

### Adding Fuel Entry

1. **Go to Trips Tab**

4. **Fill Details:**

**Required:**

- Fuel Type (Truck/Reefer)
- Truck/Reefer Number
- Fuel Quantity
- Date

**Optional:**

- Location
- Odometer Reading
- Fuel Cost
- Notes

5. **Save**

### Fuel Units

Choose your preferred unit in Settings:

- **Gallons** (US)
- **Liters** (Metric)

6. **Choose Action:**

## Tips & Tricks

### Productivity

1. **Use Share Integration**
   - Share trip details from other apps
   - Milow parses and pre-fills form

2. **Quick Add**
   - Tap + button from any screen
   - Add trip or fuel entry instantly

3. **Swipe Actions**
   - Swipe right to edit
   - Swipe left to delete
   - Faster than tapping menus

### Data Management

1. **Pull to Refresh**
   - Pull down on any list to refresh data
   - Ensures you have latest information

2. **Offline Mode**
   - App caches data locally
   - View records without internet
   - Syncs when connection restored

3. **Regular Exports**
   - Export PDFs weekly/monthly
   - Keep backup of your records
   - Easy to share with accountant

### Battery Optimization

1. **Disable Unused Features**
   - Turn off Weather if not needed
   - Disable Trucking News
   - Reduces background data usage

2. **Dark Mode**
   - Saves battery on OLED screens
   - Easier on eyes at night

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**App Won't Load Data**

- Check internet connection
- Pull to refresh
- Sign out and sign back in

**GPS Not Working**

- Enable location permissions
- Ensure GPS is on
- Try restarting app

**PDF Export Fails**

- Check storage permissions
- Ensure enough free space
- Try smaller date range

**Biometric Login Not Working**

- Re-enable in Settings
- Check device biometric settings
- Update app to latest version
